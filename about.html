Cordova Parish

The first church and convent were destroyed by the typhoon of November 25, 1877. Fr. Jose Baztan was one responsible for the reconstruction of the church made of mortar with pebbles and tabique pampango or wooden harigues. Fr. Baztan, a very holy priest who had always dreamed of volunteering for the China mission, was assassinated at the plaza of Opon by Cebuano insurgents on April 4, 1898. They threw his cadaver in the sea.

Cordova Police Station

Cordova Police Stations will maintain the coffee shop, specially in purchasing supplies. Cordova Mayor Cesar "Didoy" Suan also promise to continue supporting Cordova Police Station by providing them with their need such as gasoline. According to Police Major Michael Gingoyon, chief of Cordova Police Station, they were able to construct the coffee shop with the help of different stakeholders and the local government unit (LGU).

Cordova Municipal Hall

Cordova (also spelled Cordoba) is a municipality located in the 6th district of Cebu Province in the Philippines. Most of Cordova lies on separate islands off the southern coast of Mactan Island. It is connected to the main island of Mactan over three bridges.
